QuickFrame Marketplace

Video creation and insights platform that streamlines the original creation process, enabling a diverse set of customers to create video content for every platform, audience, and objective.

Summary

MNTN QuickFrame Marketplace is an innovative platform that connects advertisers with creators to produce high-quality video content efficiently. The marketplace aims to simplify the content creation process while maximizing collaboration and creativity. Below is an overview of the UX process and key results achieved from recent enhancements.

Process

  • User Research & Interviews: Conducted in-depth interviews with 120 advertisers and 80 creators to gather insights into their experiences and challenges. Key pain points included difficulty in finding suitable creators and a lack of clear communication tools, with 65% of users expressing frustration.
  • Persona Development & Journey Mapping: Developed 4 user personas representing different types of advertisers and creators. Mapped their journeys to identify common challenges, particularly in the onboarding and collaboration phases.
  • Wireframing & Prototyping: Designed wireframes for an improved marketplace interface that included advanced search filters, clear messaging tools, and streamlined project management features. Prototypes were tested with 25 users from both groups to gather feedback.
  • User Testing & Iteration: Conducted usability tests with 50 users, focusing on navigation, ease of communication, and project setup. Feedback led to iterative improvements, particularly in enhancing the search functionality and user onboarding experience.
