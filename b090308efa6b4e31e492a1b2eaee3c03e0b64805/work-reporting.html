Measure results in real time with a fully customizable reporting dashboard and alongside your other marketing channels in Google Analytics.

Summary

Premier Reporting is an advanced analytics tool designed to provide advertisers with clear insights into their campaign performance. The platform focuses on delivering comprehensive, yet easy-to-understand reports that drive better decision-making for advertisers. Below is the UX process and key results from the platformâ€™s enhancements.

Process

  • Research & Stakeholder Interviews: We conducted interviews with 50 advertisers to understand challenges in interpreting metrics. Main pain points included difficulty navigating reports and a need for better data visualization.
  • Persona Creation & Journey Mapping: Created 3 key personas representing beginner to advanced users. User journeys were mapped to uncover friction points, especially around accessing critical data.
  • Wireframes & Interactive Prototyping: Designed wireframes for a simplified reporting interface. 15 advertisers tested interactive prototypes, providing feedback for iterative improvements.
  • User Testing & Iteration: Conducted usability tests with 25 advertisers, focusing on ease of use and navigation. A/B testing compared user performance on the old and new dashboards.
