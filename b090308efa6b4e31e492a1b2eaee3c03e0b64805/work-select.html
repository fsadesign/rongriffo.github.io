MNTN Select

Network Premium Advertising

Summary

The MNTN Select Network is a premium advertising platform designed to enhance the user experience (UX) for advertisers seeking high-quality, targeted TV placements. Below is a summary of the UX process, including key methodologies and the results of the implementation.

Process

Our UX team followed a user-centered design approach to ensure the best experience for both advertisers and viewers. The key stages of the process included:

  • Research: We conducted interviews and surveys with 150 advertisers to identify pain points in the current platform. Additionally, we collected data from 500 viewers to gauge their interaction with TV ads.
  • Personas and User Journeys: Based on the research, we developed 5 advertiser personas and 4 viewer personas, mapping out detailed user journeys for each to understand their motivations and challenges.
  • Wireframing and Prototyping: Multiple wireframes were created, and interactive prototypes were tested with a focus group of 20 advertisers. This allowed us to iterate quickly and incorporate real-time feedback.
  • User Testing: Final prototypes were tested by 50 advertisers and 100 viewers using both usability tests and A/B testing, leading to optimized interfaces and user flows.
