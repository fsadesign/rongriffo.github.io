Premier Onboarding

Self-serve onboarding for MNTN's premier platform.

Summary

MNTN Premier self serve onboarding is a streamlined onboarding process designed to ensure a smooth transition for new advertisers onto the MNTN platform. The initiative focuses on enhancing user experience by providing comprehensive guidance and support during the initial setup phase. Below is an overview of the UX process and key results achieved from improvements made.

Process

  • User Research & Feedback Collection: Conducted interviews and surveys with 80 new advertisers to gather insights on their onboarding experience. Key issues included unclear instructions and overwhelming initial setup steps, with 70% reporting difficulties in the process.
  • Persona Development & Journey Mapping: Created 3 user personas representing various advertiser backgrounds. Mapped their onboarding journeys to identify pain points and areas needing improvement, highlighting common barriers to successful account setup.
  • Wireframing & Prototyping: Developed wireframes for a simplified onboarding interface that incorporated step-by-step guides and interactive tutorials. Prototypes were tested with 15 new advertisers to assess usability and clarity of instructions.
  • User Testing & Iterative Design: Conducted usability tests with 30 new advertisers, focusing on the ease of navigating the onboarding process. Feedback led to iterative design changes to enhance clarity and reduce confusion.
