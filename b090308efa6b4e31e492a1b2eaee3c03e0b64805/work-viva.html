VIVA Ad Generator

Create, mix, and remix elements like generative video and audio, stock footage, and your own existing assets to create videos in record time. Fast-track video creation, iteration, and exploration with ease.

Summary

MNTN VIVA Ad Generator is an AI-powered tool that enables advertisers to quickly generate high-quality video ads using customizable templates and automation. The platform aims to simplify the ad creation process while maintaining creative flexibility. Below is an overview of the UX process and the resulting outcomes from recent enhancements.

Process

  • User Research & Discovery: Conducted surveys and interviews with 100 advertisers to identify pain points in video ad creation. Key challenges included difficulties in customizing templates and navigating the editing interface. User behavior analysis showed that 60% of users abandoned the ad creation process midway.
  • Persona Development & Journey Mapping: Developed 4 user personas ranging from novice to advanced users. Mapped user journeys highlighted common barriers to successful ad creation, including unclear editing options.
  • Wireframing & Prototyping: Created wireframes for a more intuitive interface, focusing on ease of use for beginners while retaining advanced options for seasoned users. Interactive prototypes were tested with 20 advertisers, gathering feedback on usability features.
  • User Testing & Refinements: Conducted usability tests with 50 advertisers to reduce friction points in the ad creation flow, particularly around template customization and AI suggestions. A/B testing compared completion rates between old and new designs.
