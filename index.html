Life Storage Redesign

Summary

The overhaul of lifestorage.com was a game-changer in terms of user experience. Our overall goal was to deliver a modern and intuitive interface that takes convenience and usability to the next level. To improve page load speed on LifeStorage.com, we've prioritized user experience by strategically deferring the loading of below-the-fold content and other resources.

Process/Result

When I first took on the Life Storage redesign project, my primary focus was to initiate a complete overhaul of their existing website. The objective was clear: elevate the user experience, improve functionality, and boost overall effectiveness. To kickstart the process, my initial task involved streamlining and unifying four separate websites into a single, cohesive, responsive platform. By eliminating unnecessary elements and consolidating resources, we aimed to deliver a seamless and consistent experience for users.

Life Storage showed its dedication to staying ahead of the game by taking a bold step in the self-storage industry. We embraced cutting-edge solutions by being the first to adopt a fully responsive website, ditching the outdated "m-dot" approach. This game-changing move had a profound effect on how customers interacted with the website. The improved viewability made it easier for people to access the site from different devices and enjoy a seamless user experience.

Life Storage - Rent Now

Summary

Rent Now allows customers to rent storage units online quickly and conveniently. It simplifies the rental process by enabling customers to complete the entire transaction digitally, without the need for in-person visits or paperwork. Once the transaction is complete online, customers can go straight to their storage units.

Process/Result

From the get-go, we recognized that the primary hurdle we faced was the extensive information required to complete a rental, which had typically been handled in-store. Although we managed to condense certain fields, the overall process still felt heavy. Through research and brainstorming sessions, we eventually discovered a game-changing solution: organizing the form into animated sliding panels. This significantly alleviated the perceived weight of the process, providing a smoother and more user-friendly experience for our customers. Rent Now quickly evolved into the foundation of our kiosk application.

Life Storage - City/Store

Summary

The city and store templates serve as the bedrock of lifestorage.com, serving as critical components that provide customers with an extensive array of information about our facilities. Our primary objective was to enhance the current layout and introduce an intuitive solution that enables users to effortlessly access all the essential information they require.

Life Storage - Tier Pricing

Summary

At Life Storage, our tier pricing system empowers customers with a range of choices when it comes to storage units. We offer premium, standard, and value options, each with its own benefits. One of the key factors determining the pricing tiers is the proximity to an entry point. By offering these different tiers, we ensure that customers have the flexibility to select the storage unit that best suits their needs and budget.

Process/Result

We designed our pricing structure with a clear and familiar "pricing plan" theme, ensuring our customers can easily grasp the concept of having multiple tiers to choose from for each rentable space. Alongside this new feature, we introduced flexibility by offering two options: Rent Now or a traditional reservation. With Rent Now, customers can secure their space immediately, while those who prefer a more traditional approach can make a reservation and hold the space for two days without any payment required.
